The Wilson & Catherine

PB&J

Description

A wild take on a classic. PB&J is a classic.

Ingredients

  • Bread (White bread recommended but any bread will do)
  • Creamy Peanut Butter
  • Strawberry Jelly

Directions

  1. Toasting the bread is optional but highly recommended
  2. Take first slice of bread and apply peanut butter
  3. Take second sliceof bread and apply jelly
  4. Put both slices together
  5. Eat
